# 📊 Key Findings & Business Insights

## 🏥 Project Summary

Analysis of a hospital database covering 50 patients, 10 doctors,
and 200 appointments across 3 branches in 2023.
Total annual revenue: **$551,249**

---

## 👥 1. Patient Demographics & Retention

| Metric | Finding |
| -------- | --------- |
| Gender split | 62% Male, 38% Female |
| Largest age group | 31–50 years (44%) |
| Retention rate | 76% of patients have 3+ visits |
| Top insurance provider | MedCare Plus (36%) |

**💡 Insight:** The high retention rate (76%) suggests strong patient
loyalty. The 31–50 age group is the core demographic — marketing and
preventive care programs should target this segment.

---

## 🩺 2. Clinical Operations & Efficiency

| Metric | Finding |
| -------- | --------- |
| Busiest doctor | Sarah Taylor — 29 appointments |
| Highest no-show rate | David Jones — 35.7% |
| Most efficient branch | Eastside Clinic — 21% no-show rate |
| Most burdened branch | Central Hospital — 84 appointments |

**💡 Insight:** Central Hospital handles 42% of all appointments but
also has the highest no-show rate (31%). Implementing reminder systems
could recover significant lost capacity.

---

## 💊 3. Treatment & Service Insights

| Metric | Finding |
| -------- | --------- |
| Most performed treatment | Chemotherapy (24.5%) |
| Highest revenue treatment | Chemotherapy — $128,855 (23.4%) |
| Highest avg cost | MRI — $3,224 per treatment |
| Lowest revenue share | ECG — 17.5% despite high volume |

**💡 Insight:** MRI generates the highest revenue per treatment ($3,224)
but is underutilized (18% volume). Increasing MRI capacity could
significantly boost revenue.

---

## 💰 4. Financial Health & Revenue Trends

| Metric | Finding |
| -------- | --------- |
| Total annual revenue | $551,249 |
| Best month | April — $64,271 |
| Worst month | December — $27,569 |
| Paid invoices | Only 31.5% ⚠️ |
| Failed payments | 35% — highest category ⚠️ |
| Top payment method | Credit Card (37.5%) |

**💡 Insight:** Only 31.5% of billing is collected. With 35% Failed
and 33.5% Pending, the hospital has **$377,825 in uncollected revenue**
— this is the most critical operational risk identified in this analysis.

---

## ⚠️ Top Recommendations

1. **Reduce no-shows** — Implement SMS reminders, especially at
Central Hospital (31% no-show rate)
2. **Improve payment collection** — 68.5% of revenue is uncollected,
urgent follow-up system needed
3. **Expand MRI services** — Highest avg cost ($3,224) but only 18%
of treatment volume
4. **Focus on 31–50 age group** — Core demographic (44%) for
preventive care programs
